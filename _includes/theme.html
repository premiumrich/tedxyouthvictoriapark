Theme

Our event theme for this year is “Ctrl Alt Del”. The idea of Ctrl Alt Del is meant to highlight individuals who have dared to take control of their lives by deleting old, fixed-mindset based ideas and pushed their innovative, alternate ideas forward! More information regarding the meaning of this year's event theme can be found here:  http://tiny.cc/ctrlaltdel 

We believe in cultivating a community of engaged, empowered youth through the spreading of innovative ideas, and we hope that you can join us at TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark 2020!

Shifting Equilibrium

Our event theme for TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark 2019 was “Shifting Equilibrium: A Journey Into the Unknown”. “Shifting Equilibrium” is meant to represent individuals, communities, and ideas that have been (or will be) affected by some sort of vast technological, ideological, societal, or psychological change. In addition, the notion of “A Journey Into The Unknown” is meant to complement the idea of “Shifting the Equilibrium”, and is intended to represent people who dared to explore an environment that was completely unknown to them. More information regarding the underlying meaning of the event theme can be found here:  https://tiny.cc/shiftingequilibrium
