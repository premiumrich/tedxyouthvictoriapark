March 29, 2020: Update on COVID-19

Due to COVID-19, TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark 2020 will be postponed.
If you currently have a TEDxYouth@VictoriaPark 2020 ticket and are still able to attend when we have confirmed a new date, your current ticket will remain valid for the postponed event, and no changes would need to be made. Otherwise, please let us know if you would like a refund.

We will update our website when we have a new event date and you may stay in touch with us through our social media platforms.
Your health and safety is our main priority, and we want to ensure that our event will be comfortable and worry-free for you and all other participants. We apologize for any inconvenience.
Should you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to email us at info@tedxyouthvictoriapark.ca
